Historical Facts

  • Constructed in the 13th century by King Narasimhadeva I of the Eastern Ganga dynasty.

  • Dedicated to Surya, the Sun God, symbolizing time and celestial movement.
  • Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Famous for its intricate carvings and Kalinga-style architecture.
