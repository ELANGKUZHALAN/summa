Key Facts

  • Sculptor: Gutzon Borglum (primary) and later work by his son, Lincoln Borglum.
  • Construction ran from 1927 to 1941 using dynamite and careful carving techniques.
  • Each presidential face is about 60 feet (18 meters) tall carved into granite of the Black Hills.
  • Chosen presidents represent the founding, expansion, preservation, and development of the United States.
  • Located near Keystone, South Dakota, Mount Rushmore is a major American cultural landmark managed by the National Park Service.
