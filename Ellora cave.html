The Ajanta & Ellora Caves are world-famous rock-cut monuments showcasing Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain traditions. They are renowned for their paintings, sculptures, and architectural brilliance.

Historical Facts

  • Ajanta Caves date from the 2nd century BCE to 6th century CE.
  • Ellora Caves were built between the 6th and 10th centuries CE.
  • Ajanta is famous for Buddhist murals and paintings.
  • Ellora features Hindu, Buddhist, and Jain temples, including the Kailasa Temple.
